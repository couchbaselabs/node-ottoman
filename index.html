Get started

Powered by Couchbase

Couchbase is JSON database that excels in high volume transactions, making Ottoman a powerful tool to handle your data.

Fast

As Couchbase has a built-in managed cache to enable a memory-first architecture. Read and write operations run at the speed of RAM.

Familiar

Ottoman brings together the best of NoSQL document databases and relational databases.
